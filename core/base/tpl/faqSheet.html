General Answers

  • What are Points?

    Points are points awarded in recognition of your outstanding contributions. Points are redeemable for a wonderful collection of brand-name merchandise items including electronics, sporting goods, home furnishings, toys, jewelry, and travel. A variety of merchandise and travel options are available. You can access the catalog by clicking Shop on the navigation bar. ↩ Back To Top

  • When talking about Points, what do "accumulative earnings" mean?

    Points do not need to be redeemed in the amount or month in which they were issued in. They may be accumulated for awards of greater value! Points do not expire. It is recommended that you use them within 90 days after leaving the company. ↩ Back To Top

  • Where can I find more information about delivery options, damaged goods, and product specifications?

    Additional information can be found by choosing Shop from the program Web site and then browsing the online catalog ↩ Back To Top

  • How will I know how many Points I have?

    Each participant will have a personal account where Points will be deposited. There are no paper certificates to be mailed or misplaced. A personal account number is assigned to each program participant. This personal identification number (similar to a bank cash card) remains assigned to the participant throughout the life of the program. If your have a balance, it is displayed in the upper right hand corner of the program web site, under your name. ↩ Back To Top

  • Can I use cash along with Points to place an order or redeem them for cash?

    No. ↩ Back To Top

  • Can Points be given in 1/2-point increments?

    No. Points can only be given in whole-point increments. ↩ Back To Top

  • What happens if I forget my password or login ID?

    If, at any time, you forget your password or login ID, you will be able to retrieve this information using the program login screen. Click on the "Forgot Password" link to retrieve and reset your password - you must have previously answered your Security Question in order to use this feature. You can also use the Contact Us feature to work with the program administrators to confirm your information. ↩ Back To Top

  • How do I know who is eligible to participate in the program?

    Promotions for which you are eligible may have promotion rules posted online to review. You can access promotion rules by placing your cursor on the navigation bar and then selecting a promotion name. If there are rules available you will see the Review Promotion Rules option. Click on this to access these rules. ↩ Back To Top

  • Who has access to a personal account?

    Only the owner! This information is confidential and unique to each person. ↩ Back To Top

  • Where can I read about the rules of the program?

    Promotions for which you are eligible may have promotion rules posted online to review. You can access promotion rules by placing your cursor on the navigation bar and then selecting a promotion name. If there are rules available you will see the Review Promotion Rules option. Click on this to access these rules. ↩ Back To Top

  • How do I send an award?

    If you have been designated as someone who can send awards, navigate to the Recognition tab on the top navigation bar and then select the promotion name you wish to issue recognition from. The site will walk you through the issuance process. ↩ Back To Top

  • When are the points deposited?

    Points are deposited into your personal account. These deposits happen real-time, they are in your account and available for redemption as soon as they are awarded to you. ↩ Back To Top

  • When is my online statement updated?

    Your online statement is updated real-time. To view your statement, navigate to the My Info tab on the top navigation menu and then click on the My Account link. ↩ Back To Top

  • How do I redeem my Points?

    Points may be redeemed over the phone or online. To redeem online, click on the Shop tab on the top menu. Browse through the catalog at your own leisure. When you find an item you would like to redeem, click on the Add to cart button. The site will then take you through the steps to confirm your shipping address and submit your order. ↩ Back To Top

  • How do I know how many Points I need for a particular item in the catalog?

    The number of Points needed to secure each item is conveniently provided for the item. ↩ Back To Top

  • Once you place an order, how long will it take to receive the merchandise item?

    Ninety-three percent of all warehoused merchandise ordered is shipped within 24 hours. 99.3% of all awards are shipped within four weeks. Depending on where you live, merchandise will arrive within 7-10 days. Some merchandise items are not carried in inventory and must be shipped directly from the manufacturer. In such cases, shipment times for drop ship items range from 6-8 weeks. ↩ Back To Top
