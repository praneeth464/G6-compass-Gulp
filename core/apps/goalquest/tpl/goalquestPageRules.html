How it Works

  • ABC Corporation will assign each Sales Representative a base objective (the "Base Objective") for eligible sales during the program period (June 1 through August 31, 2010).
  • Each Sales Representative will have the option to select one goal from among three different Goal Levels based on the Base Objective. Each Goal Level will have a different percent of sales objective attainment, and a different reward payout after attainment of the required level of sales.
  • Goal Level must be chosen and entered on or before midnight on Friday May 28, 2010.
  • No changes to individual goal selections can be made after May 28, 2010.
  • At program end Sales Representatives who attain the required percentage achievement over objective, based on the previously selected Goal Level, will earn Points® rewards based on that Goal Level. If a Sales Representative does not achieve the required sales based on the selected Goal Level, that Agent will not receive any payout.
  • If a Sales Representative achieves a higher level of sales than the Goal Level selected, awards will be paid only for the level selected, not the higher level achieved.
